Service Process

How We Work Together

A clear, five-step process for turning a space into a finished piece of custom cabinetry — from first conversation to final installation.

  1. 01

    Consultation

    We discuss the space, your functional needs, and the overall style you're aiming for — residential, hospitality, or a larger international project.

  2. 02

    3D Design

    A detailed 3D design is prepared so you can walk through the layout, materials, and finishes before anything is built.

  3. 03

    Material & Sampling

    Once the design is approved, we confirm materials, hardware, and finishes, and prepare samples where needed.

  4. 04

    Production

    Cabinetry is manufactured to spec under quality control, with regular progress updates throughout.

  5. 05

    Delivery & Installation

    Finished pieces are shipped and installed on site, with a final walkthrough to make sure every detail matches the design.
